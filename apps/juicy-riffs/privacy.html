Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Juicy Riffs is built by Lurb Developments. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

Juicy Riffs does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device.

What Stays on Your Device

  • All projects, recordings, photos, and videos you create
  • App preferences and settings
  • Any files you import

Permissions

Camera

Used to capture photos and videos within the app. Media is saved locally to your device only.

Microphone

Used to record audio riffs and ideas. Recordings are saved locally to your device only.

Photo Library

Used only if you choose to import photos from your library or export media to your camera roll. We do not access your library without an explicit action from you.

Third Party Services

Juicy Riffs does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children

Juicy Riffs is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
