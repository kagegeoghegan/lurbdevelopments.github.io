Frequently Asked Questions

My recording won't play back

Make sure your phone is not on silent. If you recently recorded something, close and reopen the recording to reset the audio session.

Where are my files stored?

Everything is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded anywhere unless you explicitly choose to export.

How do I delete a recording or photo?

Open the item in fullscreen, tap the trash icon in the toolbar. You can also swipe left on items in some views, or use Select mode in the grid to delete multiple at once.

Can I rename a recording?

Yes. Open the item fullscreen and tap the pencil icon in the toolbar.

How do I set the Key and BPM for a project?

Open any project and tap the Key or BPM chip near the top of the screen.

How do I attach a file like a PDF or tab?

Inside a project, tap the paperclip icon to open the Files section, then tap + to import from your device.

The app asked for microphone permission and I denied it

Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone > Juicy Riffs and enable access.

Still need help?

Drop us an email and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.
