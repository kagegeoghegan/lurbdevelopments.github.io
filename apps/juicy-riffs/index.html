Features

Everything in one place.

No camera roll clutter. No voice memos buried under grocery lists. Just your music, organized.

Audio Recording

Hit record, play your riff, name it, and it is saved. Instant playback before you commit.

Video and Photo

Shoot your hands on the fretboard or snap a chord diagram. All media lives in the same project.

Projects

One project per song or idea. Keep everything grouped and findable when inspiration strikes again.

Key and BPM

Tag each project with a key and tempo so you never have to guess later.

File Attachments

Attach tabs, chord charts, PDFs and anything you need alongside your recordings.

Dark Interface

Built dark from the ground up. Easy on the eyes in any lighting.
