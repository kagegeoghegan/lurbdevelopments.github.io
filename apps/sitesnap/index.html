SiteSnap

Photo documentation for job sites and field work. Organized, exportable, and always at your fingertips.

Features

Documentation that keeps up with you.

Built for tradespeople, contractors, and anyone who needs photos organized by job, not buried in a camera roll.

Projects and Folders

Create projects for individual jobs and group them into folders by client, trade, or region. Long-press any project or folder to rename or delete it. Sort by recently active, date, name, or photo count so the job you just worked is always at the top.

Built-In Camera

Capture photos directly inside the app with support for multiple lenses, flash, and front and rear cameras. Photos land straight into the active project with GPS coordinates attached. No camera roll hunting required.

Import from Anywhere

Share photos or videos from your camera roll or any app directly into SiteSnap. Choose an existing project or create a new one on the spot. GPS data is preserved during import so every photo stays pinned to its location.

Interactive GPS Map

Every geotagged photo is plotted on an interactive map. Toggle between Streets, Satellite, and Hybrid views. Long-press anywhere to get instant driving directions, drop a pin, or draw a shape at that exact spot. Measure walking distances with the built-in ruler tool.

Site Planning and Shapes

Set a project site address to anchor a star pin on the map with an optional APE radius ring. Draw polygons, circles, and rectangles to mark boundaries, work zones, and areas of interest, each with a label, custom color, and adjustable opacity.

Route Planning

Build a multi-stop inspection route directly on the map. Tap points in the order you want to visit them. The first stop is set to your current location automatically. Save the route, then open it in Apple Maps for turn-by-turn driving directions.

Photo Annotation Tools

Draw directly on photos with shapes, lines, arrows, and text labels. Annotations are stored as a non-destructive layer so the original is always preserved. Add a caption or note to any photo. Notes appear in exports.

Notes and File Attachments

Write project-wide notes for the whole job. Attach PDFs, specs, drawings, and documents to any project alongside your photos. Files are stored with the project and can be shared or exported at any time.

Export and Share

Select any photos and package them into a ZIP for fast, lossless delivery. Or push directly to Google Drive or Microsoft OneDrive with a single tap.

iCloud Backup

All project data is automatically stored in iCloud Drive when you are signed into iCloud. Your projects are available on every device signed into the same Apple ID, and always safe if you switch phones.
