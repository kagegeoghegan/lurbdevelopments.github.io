Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

SiteSnap is built by Lurb Developments. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

Data We Collect

SiteSnap does not collect or sell any personal data. All project data, photos, and files remain on your device unless you choose to sync them to a cloud service.

What Stays on Your Device

  • All projects, photos, videos, and file attachments
  • GPS coordinates attached to photos (stored locally only)
  • App preferences and settings

Cloud Services (Optional)

SiteSnap offers optional integration with Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, and iCloud Drive. These are strictly opt-in. When you choose to sync, files are sent directly to your own cloud account. Lurb Developments does not have access to your cloud storage or credentials.

Permissions

Camera

Used to capture photos and videos on site. Media is saved to your device.

Location

Used to tag photos with GPS coordinates. Location is stored locally with each photo and is never transmitted to our servers.

Photo Library

Used only when you import from or export to your camera roll, triggered by an explicit action from you.

Third Party Services

SiteSnap does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children

SiteSnap is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
