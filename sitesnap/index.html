Features

Documentation that keeps up with you.

Built for tradespeople, contractors, and anyone who needs photos organized by job, not buried in a camera roll.

Project-Based Organization

Create a project for each job or site. Every photo and video lives where it belongs, searchable and sortable, never lost in your camera roll.

Built-In Camera with Video

Capture photos and videos directly inside the app. Supports multiple lenses, flash control, and front and rear cameras. Files go straight into the active project.

GPS Map View

Every photo is automatically geotagged and plotted on an interactive map. Tap any pin to see the photo taken at that location. Grid thumbnails show matching pin numbers so you always know where a shot came from.

Site Plan Overlay

Upload a floor plan or site plan image, pin it to an address, and overlay it on the map alongside your geotagged photos for a complete picture of the job.

Photo Annotation Tools

Draw directly on photos with shapes, lines, arrows, and text labels in red, black, or white. Annotations are saved as a non-destructive layer so the original photo is always preserved.

Notes and File Attachments

Add a text note to any individual photo, or write project-wide notes for the whole job. Attach PDFs, specs, and documents to a project alongside your media.

PDF Export and ZIP Sharing

Select any photos and export a clean PDF report to share with clients or your team. Or package the originals into a ZIP file for fast, lossless delivery.

Cloud Sync and Batch Tools

Sync selected photos to Google Drive or Microsoft OneDrive with a single tap. Batch rename files with a custom prefix and numbering sequence, or move photos between projects at any time.
