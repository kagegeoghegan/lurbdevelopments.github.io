Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change the key or scale?

Tap the Key or Scale buttons in the control panel at the bottom of the screen. A full list picker will open. Select your choice and the fretboard updates instantly.

What are positions?

Positions filter the fretboard to a specific positional pattern within a fret window. Select "All" to see the full scale across the neck, or choose a numbered position (1 through 5) to focus on one area at a time.

How do I see note names instead of intervals?

Tap the Intervals / Notes button on the left side of the control panel to cycle between interval numbers, note names, and plain dots.

Can I mute individual strings?

Yes. Tap any string peg in the tuning column on the left edge of the fretboard to toggle that string on or off.

How do I learn about a scale's chords and theory?

Tap the Now Playing bar (the pill showing your current key and scale). This opens a panel with a description of the scale, common chord progressions, and a scale degree table, all transposed to your selected key.

A scale or chord looks wrong

We want to know. Email us with the specific scale, root note, and what you believe is incorrect and we will look into it right away.

The app is not behaving as expected

Try force-quitting and reopening the app. If the issue persists, send us a note at the email below describing what happened and we will look into it.
